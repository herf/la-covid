Long term care facilities (LTCF) are included on this list if they had at least one resident who developed symptoms while in the facility and tested positive for COVID-19. This list is intended to inform the public of the current COVID-19 situation in LTCFs in Los Angeles County. Inclusion on this list does not suggest neglect or wrongdoing on the part of the facility.
